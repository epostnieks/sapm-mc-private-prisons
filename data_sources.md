# Data Sources — Private Prisons Monte Carlo Simulation

Channel parameters in `mc_simulation.py` trace to the sources listed here.
The paper (Private Prisons: Occupancy Guarantee Trap) is available on SSRN and contains the full
reference list and §4 calibration narrative.

---

## Channel Sources

### `C1_over_incarceration`

Lobbying for longer sentences, mandatory minimums. Private prison occupancy guarantees drive over-incarceration. Sources: Sentencing Project, Justice Policy Institute.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C2_human_capital_destruction`

Recidivism, intergenerational transmission of incarceration. Lost earnings, family disruption. Sources: Pew, Vera Institute.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C3_monopoly_captive_extraction`

Price gouging on phone calls, commissary, medical co-pays in captive market. Sources: Prison Policy Initiative, FCC.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C4_institutional_defense_failure`

Inadequate healthcare, understaffing, violence. DOJ consent decrees. Sources: DOJ Civil Rights Division, ACLU.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

### `C5_governance_political_feedback`

Campaign contributions, ALEC model legislation, revolving door. Sources: Justice Policy Institute, OpenSecrets.

*Full citations: paper §4 (available SSRN).*

## Industry Revenue (Π)

Source: paper §2 and §4. See paper §DA-1 (Decision Audit Field 7) for full
